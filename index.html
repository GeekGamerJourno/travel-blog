Visiting South Africa

In March 2017 I travelled to South Africa to attend the International Federation of Agriculture Journalists conference in Pretoria and Cape Town.

Kruger National Park

Our first stop was Kruger National Park. We spent three days on safari, watching wildlife.

IFAJ Congress

The IFAJ congress saw journalists from countries all over the world tour South African farms.

Scenes of Africa

The landscapes of South Africa are breath-taking.

Pretoria

Nelson Mandella embraces Pretoria from the government buildings, encouraging inclusion and progress.

Village Life

Tourist attractions preserve parts of village life in Africa

Nightime Magic

Africa is beautiful by day and stunning by night.

Wine Country

Near Cape Town, the vineyards sprawl up to the feet of mountains and the wine flows freely.

Free Trade Education

Free trade wineries provide vital education and support for children and their families in South Africa.

Coastal Cape Town

A seaside city of awe-inspiring beauty, Cape Town is a playgroud for the rich and famous.
